Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?

ShopifyGPT Pro is built specifically for Shopify with native integration, bulk processing, and GPT-4 Vision that analyzes product images. Generic AI tools require manual copy-pasting and can't understand visual product details like colors, materials, or style.

Will the generated content hurt my SEO rankings?

No. Our AI creates unique, contextual content for each product, solving the duplicate content problem that dropshippers face. Plus, we optimize for Shopify-specific SEO including meta titles, descriptions, and alt tags.

How long does it take to process products?

Individual products take 30-60 seconds. Bulk processing of 100 products typically completes in under 30 minutes. Much faster than writing manually or using generic tools that require individual prompts.

Can I maintain my brand voice?

Yes! Our brand voice training analyzes your best-performing product pages to learn your tone, style, and messaging. The AI then generates new content that matches your established brand voice.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. If you're not seeing better results than manual writing or other tools, we'll refund your money. We also offer unlimited revisions on the Growth plan and above.
